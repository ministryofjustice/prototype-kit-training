Filters

Filters are tools that help users narrow down large sets of information by selecting specific criteria—such as date, category, status, or keyword.

They are especially useful in systems with complex or high-volume data, like case management platforms, search interfaces, or dashboards. By applying filters, users can quickly find relevant information, reduce clutter, and improve decision-making.

This leads to greater efficiency, better user experience, and more accurate outcomes.
