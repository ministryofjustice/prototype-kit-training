    • Tutorial one - Creating, editing and linking pages

    Now it is time to start building out the prototype and the first thing is to create some pages.



    The first thing to do is to click on the Manage your prototype link. This will take you to area for managing and creating different areas of your prototype.

    In the section named Templates section there is a list of pages you can quickly add into your prototype. By selecting view you are able to get a look of how they will be when added into your prototype. When clicking create, enter the name of the page you then want to create. This page will then appear automatically in your code editor. Go ahead and create four pages, three question pages and then one check answers page.

    Now the structure for a mini service is set up we can begin to change the default content and also link the pages together.

    The content, which the prototype gives as a starter will need to be edited to suit your needs. The form action on the page should be action="/prototype-kit-training-page-2"
